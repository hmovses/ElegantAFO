Crow Boot

Full Immobilization Walker

The Charcot Restraint Orthotic Walker (CROW) is a heavily padded, hard-shelled walker that fully restricts movement in the ankle and foot to protect and prevent further injury or trauma. Recommended for neuropathic patients with severe foot and ankle deformity.

Clinical Indications

  • Neuropathic ulcerations due to diabetes
  • Charcot deformities of the foot and/or ankle
  • Calcaneal or toe amputations
  • Trauma
  • Pre- or post-surgical intervention of mid and hind foot

Product Details

  • Heavily padded, hard-shelled walker design
  • Fully restricts ankle and foot movement
  • Customizable per the practitioner’s discretion
  • Available exclusively through licensed medical providers

Suggested L Codes

  • L4631 – Ankle foot orthosis, walking boot type, varus/valgus correction, rocker bottom, anterior tibial shell, soft interface, custom arch support, plastic or other material, includes straps and closures, custom fabricated

  • Severe pronation or pes planus
